Game Info

  • Game flow: You are player 1 (human) playing against player 2 (AI).
  • Making a move: Simply click on a valid space, player 1 (you) always goes first. Sometimes you may have no moves after an AI move, in which case it moves twice. Likewise, the AI may not have a move after you, in which case you can move again. On higher/slow difficulties a spinner will appear when the AI is thinking.
  • Start Over: Start a new game with a different AI Level
  • On-Screen Score: A simple count of squares for each player
  • Final Score: Your on-screen score weighted by difficulty using a secret formula :)
  • Gameplay notes: When you select a new game after game over it will automatically use the AI level of the previous game. Click "Start Over" to change the level. Warning: Higher difficulties (especially hard) may be slow on particular phone models.
  • Top Scores: Find out your top 10 best (final) scores! Only winning scores are added.
  • Rules: Reversi is a simple game that's somewhat hard to explain. For a good overview, check Wikipedia.
